About Us

Rodeo Tickets.com offers tickets to all Rodeo events in the US and internationally. You will be able to purchase your Rodeo tickets here online at our site. We also offer great Customer Service here online as well. Feel free to chat directly with any of our ticket agents while you are here and ask any questions you may need. We accept all the major credit cards. You may also purchase your Rodeo tickets via our free 800-298-7762 telephone number. All ticket prices are in US Currency. Ticket prices and availability are subject to change at any time. A confirmation receipt will be issued upon full payment.

Rodeo Tickets.com reserves the right to provide upgrades on all ticket orders at no extra charge to the customer. Ticket deliveries are guaranteed no later than noon on the day of the event unless stated otherwise, however every effort will be made to deliver tickets as early as possible. All tickets will be delivered by federal express overnight unless stated otherwise.

Rodeo Tickets.com will not be responsible for any weather delays, event postponements, event cancellations, change of venue, date, time, lost or stolen tickets.
